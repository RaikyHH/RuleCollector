How Decay Works

The Decay Score (0–100) is shown in every rule's detail panel under Last Updated. It is computed from the rule's last_updated_at timestamp (falling back to first_seen_at if no update exists).

  • 100 — freshly updated, no age concerns.
  • ~70+ — within warning threshold, still active.
  • ~40–70 — approaching greyout, analyst review advised.
  • 0 — at hide threshold, rule would be suppressed by default.

The score decreases linearly from 100 at day 0 to 0 at the Hide threshold. It does not depend on rule status (stable/experimental) — only on time since last change.

To exempt a specific rule from decay effects, click the ○ Pin No Decay button in its detail panel. The exemption is stored in your browser's localStorage.
